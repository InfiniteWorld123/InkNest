Taxonomy — tags/categories are simple tables with basic list, lookup, and tag creation.

Notifications — list notifications, mark one/all as read, delete.

Users — profile lookup and profile update.

Engagement — likes, bookmarks, follows, and post views; several related tables and authentication rules.

Comments — comments, replies, ownership, nested relationships, and cascading behavior.

Posts — hardest because it includes ownership, slugs, statuses, taxonomy relationships, publishing, filtering, and CRUD.
